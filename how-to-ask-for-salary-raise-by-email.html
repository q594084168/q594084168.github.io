Frequently Asked Questions

How do I follow up after a job application without being annoying?

Wait 5-7 business days after submitting your application. Keep your follow-up brief: reference the position title, reiterate your interest, and ask if there's anything else you can provide. Avoid phrases like 'just checking in' — they add no value.

What's the ideal length for a professional email?

Five sentences or fewer is the gold standard. If you need more, use bullet points. People scan emails on their phones — if your key message isn't visible in the preview pane, it probably won't be read.

How long should I wait before following up after an interview?

Send a thank-you email within 24 hours. If you haven't heard back and the timeline they gave has passed, wait an additional 3-5 business days before following up. Keep it gracious, not pushy.

What tone should I use when asking for a raise?

Be confident but not entitled. Frame the request around the value you've delivered — use specific metrics if possible. Schedule a dedicated meeting rather than slipping it into a casual conversation. Practice your talking points beforehand.
