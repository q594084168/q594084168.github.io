How to Write a Great Tool Message

  1. 1Paste your complete message — the AI works best with full context including greeting, body, and closing.
  2. 2Be specific about what you want to change. 'Make it more professional' works; 'make it better' is too vague.
  3. 3Review the output and adjust. AI suggestions are a starting point — add your personal touch and specific details.

FAQ

How short is too short?

The goal is to communicate effectively, not hit a word count. If your message is clear in 3 sentences, send 3 sentences.

What's the most common filler to remove?

'I just wanted to,' 'I was wondering if,' 'Sorry to bother you but.' These phrases add no value and undermine your authority.

Do short emails seem rude?

No — short, clear emails are appreciated by busy professionals. What seems rude is being vague, demanding, or dismissive, not being concise.
