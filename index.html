Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI Tone Modifier?

An AI Tone Modifier rewrites your text while keeping the original meaning but adjusting the tone — making it sound more professional, friendly, polite, or any other style you need.

Yes. The core tool is completely free with no sign-up required. We may offer premium features in the future, but the tone conversion will always be free.

Does the AI change the meaning of my message?

No. The AI is designed to preserve your original meaning and intent while adjusting only the tone, wording, and emotional register of your message.

Can I make my message sound more professional?

Yes. Paste your message above and the tool will generate a polished, boardroom-ready version that maintains your original message.
