Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI Tone Modifier? +

An AI Tone Modifier rewrites your text while keeping the original meaning but adjusting the tone — making it sound more professional, friendly, polite, or any other style you need. Unlike generic writing tools, it preserves your intent and specific details.

Can I rewrite emails into different tones? +

Yes. Paste your email or message and the tool generates versions in Professional, Friendly, Polite, Formal, Casual, Confident, Persuasive, and Empathetic tones. Choose the one that fits your situation.

Is ToneModifier free? +

Yes. The core tool is completely free with no sign-up required. There are no hidden fees, usage limits, or premium features that block basic functionality. We may offer advanced features in the future, but tone conversion will always be free.

Does the AI change the meaning of my message? +

No. The AI is designed to preserve your original meaning and intent while adjusting only the tone, wording, and emotional register. Your message, key points, and requests remain intact — just delivered more effectively.
