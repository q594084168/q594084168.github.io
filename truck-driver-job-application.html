FAQ

What should truck drivers emphasize?

Safety record, years of experience, types of vehicles operated, route experience, and reliability. These are what fleet managers care about most.

How formal should the email be?

Direct and professional — skip corporate buzzwords. Honest, straightforward communication builds more trust in trade industries.

What certifications should I mention?

CDL class and endorsements, hazmat certification, clean driving record, and any specialized equipment training.
