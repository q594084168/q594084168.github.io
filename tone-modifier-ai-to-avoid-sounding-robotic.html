How to Write a Great Auto Message

  1. 1Consider your audience — their role, relationship to you, and communication style preferences.
  2. 2State your purpose clearly in the first sentence. Don't bury the ask or key information.
  3. 3Use appropriate formality. When in doubt, err slightly more formal — you can always relax the tone in follow-ups.
  4. 4Proofread before sending. A single typo can undermine an otherwise professional message.
