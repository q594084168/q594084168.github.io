FAQ

What makes an email sound professional?

Clear structure, specific subject lines, confident language without hedging, and appropriate formality for the recipient.

How do I make my emails more confident?

Remove hedging words like 'just,' 'maybe,' and 'sorry to bother you.' Replace with direct, action-oriented language.

Can this help with non-native English speakers?

Yes — the tool is especially useful for non-native speakers who want to ensure their tone matches professional expectations.
