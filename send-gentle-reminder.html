FAQ

How do I phrase a reminder without nagging?

Add value or context — don't just repeat the original request. Mention a deadline, share a relevant update, or offer help.

How many reminders is too many?

Two reminders after the initial request is reasonable. After three total messages, pick up the phone or accept the silence as an answer.

What's the best subject line for a reminder?

'Quick follow-up on [specific topic]' or 'Checking in: [project name] update needed by [date].' Be specific and actionable.
