Why Salary Negotiation Email Matters

Salary conversations are among the most stressful professional interactions. Research shows that people who negotiate their salary earn an average of $1 million more over their careers than those who don't. Yet most people avoid these conversations because they don't know how to phrase them professionally. The right words can make the difference between getting what you deserve and leaving money on the table.

FAQ

Is it OK to negotiate salary over email?

Yes, especially for remote positions. Email gives you time to present market data and craft your argument carefully.

How much should I counter-offer?

Research market rates for your role and location. A counter-offer of 10-15% above the initial offer is typical.

What if they rescind the offer?

This is extremely rare when you negotiate professionally. Employers expect negotiation and budget for it.
